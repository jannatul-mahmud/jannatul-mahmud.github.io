Hi, I'm Jannatul

About me.

I'm a Computer Engineering undergrad at Wright State University, building toward a career in embedded systems and software engineering.

I came to Ohio from Dhaka, Bangladesh, started at Sinclair Community College where I earned an Associate Degree in Computer Science, then transferred to Wright State to finish my B.S. in Computer Engineering. I'll graduate in December 2027.

My interest is the layer where software meets hardware. I've built FSM-based traffic controllers in both Logisim digital logic and ESP32 firmware, written LC-3 assembly programs that pass parameters via the runtime stack, and explored quantum gates in Qiskit. I like projects where I get to debug all the way down to the wire.

Outside class, I help run a small phone repair business in Dayton — which has taught me as much about customer service, problem diagnosis, and shipping work on a deadline as any class has.

I'm currently looking for internships in software, embedded systems, or hardware/software support.
